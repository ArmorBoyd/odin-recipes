Adobo

Adobo is an iconic dish that represents Filipino food worldwide. It is always present and usually takes the top place in all the Filipino food list you can find.

Ingredients

  • Pork Belly
  • Soy Sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Bay leaves
  • Peppercorn
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Oil

Steps on How to Cook Adobo

  1. Marinate your protein

    2. Combine the pork belly, soy sauce, and garlic then marinade for at least 1 hour

  2. Cook your protein

    3. Heat the pot and put-in the marinated pork belly. Cook this all up for a few minutes

  3. Simmer your pork

    4. Pour the remaining marinade including the garlic. Add water, whole peppercorn, and dried bay leaves. Then bring your mixture to a boil. Simmer for 40 minutes to 1 hour

  4. Finishing Touches

    5. Put the vinegar inside and simmer for 12 to 15 minutes. Add more soy sauce and sugar to taste. Sugar is optional.

  5. Serve hot and Enjoy!
